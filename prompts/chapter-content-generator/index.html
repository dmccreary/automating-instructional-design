    Begin with a compelling statement that Specification-Driven Design (SDD) is a powerful design process that focuses on teaching instructional designers to write crystal-clear high-quality specifications for each MicroSim. The key is to focus on WHAT the microsim should do, but leave it up to the LLMs to decide HOW to implement the MicroSim. Create several full examples of MicroSim specification files and describe how they avoided ambiguity of the design and also used specific language to make clear how the MicroSim should behave.
